# 📖 About AI Hedge Fund

## 🎯 Vision

**AI Hedge Fund** is built on a single question: **What if you could put the greatest investment minds in history in one room and have them debate every stock?**

v3.0 makes that vision real — 12 legendary investors with fully distilled mental models form a formal investment committee for every analysis, producing consensus recommendations through structured debate.

---

## 🧬 Philosophy: The Nüwa Distillation Framework

### Why "Distillation" Rather Than "Imitation"?

Imitation copies surface behavior. Distillation extracts the **essential structure** of a master's thinking from their **entire public record** — books, papers, interviews, videos, letters, speeches, tweets:

- **Mental Models**: How do they see the world? What frameworks do they use?
- **Decision Heuristics**: Under what conditions do they act? What are the thresholds?
- **Expression DNA**: How do they speak? What sentence patterns? What signature vocabulary?
- **Anti-Patterns**: What would they NEVER do? What behaviors are outside their playbook?
- **Honesty Boundaries**: What blind spots do they admit to? What can't they do?

A fully distilled agent can produce, for any investment question, the response that master would **likely** give given the same information — complete with reasoning process, expressed uncertainty, and decision patterns.

## 🔥 Why v3.0 Matters

### The Problem with v1.0-v2.0

Earlier versions had "agents" but no "committee":
- Limited number of agents (5-9)
- No systematic debate protocol
- Incomplete personality representations (Dalio and Wood were placeholders)
- No consensus-building mechanism

### v3.0 Breakthroughs

1. **12-Member Committee**: Covers value, macro, quant, innovation, activist — a complete opinion spectrum on every stock

2. **Formal Debate Protocol**: Not simple vote aggregation — structured debate flow, cross-domain challenges, formalized consensus building

3. **Dalio & Wood Full Personalities**: Deep distillation from complete public records — not "tone imitation" but replication of thinking frameworks, decision processes, expression styles, and blind-spot awareness

4. **22-Master Distillation Library**: Code-level implementation of 22 masters — 12 core + 15 on-demand

## 🏗️ Technical Principles

### 1. Open & Transparent
All research models, valuation methods, and decision flows are open-source. If ARK can open-source their models, so can we.

### 2. Irreplaceability
Each agent has a unique and irreplaceable perspective. 12 agents are not 12 "similar opinions" — they represent fundamentally different investment philosophies.

### 3. Adversarial Thinking
Real investment insight comes from constructive conflict. Our debate protocol is not designed to seek harmony — it's designed to find the weakest link in every argument.

### 4. Auditability
Every agent's conclusion has a clear reasoning chain. You can trace any recommendation back to which agent, what reasoning, what mental model.
